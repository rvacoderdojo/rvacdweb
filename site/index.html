About RVA Coder Dojo

RVA Coder Dojo is a free, volunteer led, open source resource to help kids, ages 5-17, understand how to become digital producers.

Most children are outstanding digital consumers. Very few understand how the technology they consume is created. We seek to introduce and inspire kids to learn and explore programming, digital and technology design.

Computer science develops studentsâ€™ computational and critical thinking skills and shows them how to create, not simply use, new technologies. This fundamental knowledge is needed to prepare students for the 21st century, regardless of their ultimate field of study or occupation.

We embrace the philosophy of "failing forward". We believe that through both success and failure, we create the foundations for knowledge.

Upcoming Sessions (01/17)


 

Scratch for Beginners

Experience Level: Beginner

Recommended Ages: 7-12

This session will be geared towards learning the basics of Scratch programming. Scratch is a visually oriented language for kids to learn programming concepts. During this session we will introduce Scratch programming through a series of very basic challenges.

This class is recommended for children who have never used Scratch before, or those who want a more basic introduction to using Scratch.

Intro to Java with Robocode (first steps towards Minecraft Modding)

Experience Level: Intermediate (some prior coding)

Recommended Ages: 9-16

Note: This is a slightly more advanced session. It is recommended that participants have had some exposure to programming before (Scratch counts!)

This session will be our first foray in to Java and the first stepping stone in learning how to code Minecraft mods. Using Robocode we will begin to introduce the basics of the Java language (which Minecraft is written in) and get children familiar with some of the concepts they will need later on to develop Minecraft mods. Robocode is an easy to setup and fun environment to begin to explore Java. In this session the children will use Robocode to construct their own virtual robotic battle tank, then the tanks will be put in a virtual arena to battle to the finish.

The Hour of Code

Experience Level: Beginner - Experienced

Recommended Ages: 5-99

Whether your child has never seen a line of code, or already has their own app in the App Store, there will be an activity here for them. We will provide you with many different options for self-paced learning and exploration of code and coding concepts. There will be options for a very wide variety of ages and levels of experience including some programming options favorable to early/pre-readers. This will not be a lecture but a self guided session (with tools that make it easy to find your own way).

Note: We have new content including a "Frozen" challenge!

We will have experienced, professional programmers as mentors available during the "Hour of Code" to offer suggestions and help you if you get stuck. As always in the Coder Dojo, we will encourage the participants to help each other out as well. We often learn best by teaching others.
